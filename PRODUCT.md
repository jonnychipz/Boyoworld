# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

BOYO listeners, social followers, gamers, and visitors discovering BOYOWORLD through music, clips, merchandise, or the game.

## Product Purpose

BOYOWORLD is a public promotional universe built around one flagship third-person browser game. The opening view combines the BOYOWORLD identity, game cabinet, music, social and merch routes. Playing turns mounted BOYO, the exact white-mask marks, videos, Banshees signal, sayings and visual language into an explorable, star-heavy violet night city.

Success means visitors immediately recognise BOYOWORLD, understand the arrow/Space controls, explore the city, defeat 25 of the 50 surreal enemies, collect BOYO coins, discover proximity signal stations (each representing an official track), watch the in-cabinet reward, and continue to an official BOYO destination.

Deep-link station warp buttons (in the Banshees, world and music sections) start the game and teleport BOYO directly to the matching signal station, turning content browsing into immediate play.

## Brand Personality

Punk-chaotic, confrontational, funny, Welsh, surreal, and professionally constructed.

## Design Principles

1. **One world, one unmistakable identity.**
2. **Chaos around the game; clarity inside it.**
3. **BOYO must read as the supplied knitted-mask character from every camera angle.**
4. **Music lives inside the city through proximity, not as a detached playlist.**
5. **Every object must distinguish itself through silhouette, light, label, or motion.**
6. **Promotion is part of play: billboards, landmarks, rewards, social tiles and merch.**
7. **Mounted motion must read instantly:** the wooden toy horse and posed rider share a stable gameplay root while remaining visibly articulated.
8. **Defeats speak in-world:** BOYO's comic callout is immediate, temporary, reduced-motion safe and announced accessibly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for the surrounding site. Support arrow-key play, touch controls, pinch zoom, visible focus, high-contrast guidance, sound controls, pause/restart, reduced motion, fullscreen touch controls and non-audio feedback. Kill phrases are repeated through the game live region, and reduced mode removes speech-bubble flourish while preserving its readable duration.
