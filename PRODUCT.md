# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

BOYO listeners, music-video viewers, social followers, gamers, and new visitors discovering the artist. They arrive for a fast, memorable promotional experience that rewards play with official music.

## Product Purpose

BOYOWORLD is a promotional music universe with a five-level browser game at its centre. It turns BOYO's distinctive white balaclava, Welsh identity, underground visual language, music catalogue, merchandise, and social channels into one coherent, replayable destination.

Success means visitors immediately recognise BOYO, understand a level's exact win condition before danger begins, see their progress throughout play, complete a level, watch the automatically started music reward, and continue to an official BOYO destination.

## Brand Personality

Punk-chaotic, confrontational, and mischievous, but constructed with the precision of a professional game title. The voice is short, Welsh, playful, and quotable rather than corporate.

## Anti-references

- Generic neon cyberpunk dashboards
- Childish mascot platformers
- Corporate music landing pages
- Unstructured visual noise that harms gameplay
- Template card grids and generic gaming gradients
- Copycat recreations of music-video frames without a clear game purpose

## Design Principles

1. **Chaos in the frame, clarity in the controls.** The world can feel torn, loud, and unstable while every action remains readable.
2. **BOYO is always unmistakable.** The white knit mask, eye openings, mouth opening, Welsh dragon, and compact black outfit stay consistent in every genre.
3. **Play unlocks music.** Every win becomes an immediate bridge into an official BOYO video.
4. **Promotion is part of the world.** Social, streaming, and merchandise links appear as posters, transmissions, and physical artefacts rather than a detached link list.
5. **Five levels, five genres, one universe.** Each level changes the interaction model without changing the character or brand language.
6. **Never make the player guess.** Every world states how to win, the relevant controls, the target count, and current progress.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard, pointer, and touch input; visible focus; high-contrast game information; concise instructions; sound controls; pause/restart; reduced-motion preferences; and a non-audio cue for every audio cue.
